Ryder Crap History

2008 - Present

The Ryder Crap has become one of the world’s greatest sporting events. Every year, 16 of the best players from the South and the North go head-to-head in match play competition. Drama, tension, incredible golf, camaraderie, sportsmanship and alcohol consumption are served nearly in equal measures, captivating an audience of about 3 people around the world. It’s an event that transcends sport, yet remains true to the spirit of its founder, Richard Blake.
